MASS EXTINCTION: Coming In Hot & Fast

A map focused on the increased number of threatened species in current years

About This Map

This map was created to highlight the dramatic increase the in number of threatened species. It was inspired by the writings of Elizabeth Kolbert in "The Sixth Extinction". The information displayed is from the Red List of Threatened Species publication sponsored by the World Conservation Union (IUCN).
